How the math works
3-way / 2-way back-hedge: set each opposite-leg stake so that the return on each outcome equals the original side's return. Stake on outcome i = (orig_stake × orig_odds) ÷ outcome_i_odds. Total cost = sum of stakes. Locked profit = (orig_stake × orig_odds) − total_cost. Same profit regardless of which outcome lands.

Betfair lay: lay_stake = (orig_stake × orig_odds) ÷ (lay_odds − commission_factor). Lay liability = lay_stake × (lay_odds − 1). Locked profit ≈ orig_stake × orig_odds − orig_stake − lay_liability + lay_stake × (1 − commission).

The number of pounds you actually have at risk is the total of all stakes (3-way/2-way) or original stake + lay liability (Betfair). Calculator shows both.
